Christi Savino

Christi is a Biblical Counselor and has a passion for encouraging, discipling, and teaching others to live life knowing they were created for more than just living for the weekend. She has a Masters of Biblical Counseling and loves to study the Bible to know and understand God through her relationship with His Son Jesus. She has seen lives changed and marriages healed when someone takes that important step of faith and determines to live not in their own strength but in the power of Jesus Christ. She is a wife and mother. Her 5 kids and 3 grandkids keep her very happy and busy in North Carolina.
